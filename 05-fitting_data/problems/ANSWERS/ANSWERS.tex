\section{Problem 1 - Calculating the Speed of Light}
\label{sec:prob1}

We are given the following data from experiments measuring the speed of light and we are asked to calculate the weighted average and its accompanying internal and external errors:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Bergstrang (1948) (299 793 \(\pm 2.0\)) km/s
    \item Essen et al. (1947) (29 792 \(\pm\) 4.5) km/s
    \item Jones (1947) (299 782 \(\pm\) 25) km/s
\end{itemize}
